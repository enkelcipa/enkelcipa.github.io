Get Scrapped!

Get Scrapped! is a mutliplayer party game built in Unreal Engine 5.6 for my Senior Collaborative Games Development unit at University. It features 3 distinct phases where players scavenge for parts, assemble a bot and then fight in the arena to be the last one standing.
This was a group project consisting of 20+ students in my group. I was the Design Lead for this module and was responsible for overseeing my team of junior designers and communicating between other disciplines.

View GitHub Repository

Team Oversight

My role as a lead was to assign tasks to my junior designers, oversee and approve designs before handing them over to the tech team. I had to ensure that the quality of designs and diagrams were to a high standard to ensure easy implementation from the tech team. I worked in tandem with other teams and leads to ensure that all members had what they needed to complete their tasks.

Technical Implementations

I implemented the Duplicate Part Stat Increase System, which rewarded players for collecting multiple of the same part by increasing a random stat on that part by 2% for each additional part collected.
I worked very closely with the Tech Lead to fix bugs on a weekly basis, this included but was not limited to Controller ID fixes, UI fixes and tweaks and communication between levels.

Asset Implementation

During the final sprint to push the game to completion, Production created the "Mech Strike Team". I was not initially part of this team, but I volunteered to assist them as they had no one to import assets into Unreal.
I worked closely with one of our 3D artists to ensure that textures for various models were imported and were visually correct within the engine.
I also imported various other textures from the Concept team to create decals for use throughout the levels.
