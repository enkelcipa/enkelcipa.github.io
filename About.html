About Me

Hello! My name is Enkel. I am a Gameplay and Technical Designer who is graduating from the University of Staffordshire with a degree in Gameplay Design and Production.
I have a passion for video games both playing and making them. I enjoy the designing and implementation of complex, in depth systems and mechanics.

My focus is on designing and building modular gameplay systems to allow for efficient development. I enjoy taking designs from initial concepts to advanced implementation, including but not limited to combat mechanics, progression systems and player abilities.
I have spent the last 3 years honing my skills in Unreal Engine 5 and am wanting to branch out to other engines. As a Technical Designer, I aspire to learn as many programming languages as I can to ensure that I can work in many environments in order to create prototypes and implement designs as efficiently as possible.

During my time at University, I have worked on various projects, both in solo and group settings, making games of various genres and as I begin my career in the games industry, I am eager to contribute to projects that create engaging and memorable experiences for players whilst growing as both a designer and developer.
