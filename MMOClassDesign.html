MMO Class Design

This is a passion project where I create a single player MMO framework inside Unreal Engine 5.6 with the goal being to design and implement three classes using Final Fantasy 14 as my inspiration for the design philosophy. For this project I intend to design and implement one tank, one healer and one dps class.

Healer

My healer class is the first class I designed. It is my personal take on the Scholar job in Final Fantasy 14 leaning much heavier on the Battle Tactician theme that this job tries to take on. A Battle Tactician is magical healer that adjusts their approach to an encounter based on the situation at hand. They manipulate various resources to execute abilities called Tactics, Measures and Paradigms and can manipulate their healing spells to either apply a shield at the cost of healing power or apply a stronger heal.
