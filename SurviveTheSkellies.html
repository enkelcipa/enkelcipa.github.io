Survive the Skellies

Survive the Skellies is a Survivors-like game built in Unreal Engine 5.6 for my C++ for Designers unit at University. We were tasked with creating a game of our choosing utilising C++. Hexpansion is a city builder game built in Unreal Engine 5.6 for my Prototyping for Technical Designers unit at University. We were tasked to pick a genre from a list, then produce a visually and technically polished game within 8 weeks.

Hexpansion features a hexagonal grid map with procedurally generated biomes. I designed and implemented systems for building placement, destruction and a responsive road placement system that automatically adjusts to the correct mesh and orientation.
